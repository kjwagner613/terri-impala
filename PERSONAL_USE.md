# ImpalaStreamer — Free Personal Use

This document explains what “Free Personal Use” means for the open‑repository version of ImpalaStreamer.

## What You Can Do
You may use the open‑repository version of ImpalaStreamer for personal, non‑commercial purposes, including:

- personal media management  
- hobby projects  
- individual experimentation  
- non‑monetized workflows  
- private forks and modifications  

If you can fork the repo and get it working for your own personal use, you are welcome to do so.

## What You Cannot Do
Personal-use rights do **not** include:

- redistribution of your fork  
- publishing or mirroring the software  
- packaging it as a product  
- using it in a business or organization  
- using it in monetized workflows  
- creating a competing product  
- using DD branding, microservices, or catalog systems  

## Why This Exists
The open-repo version is intended for developers and explorers.  
The packaged retail version is intended for the general market.

GitHub is a small technical audience; the consumer market is significantly larger.  
This model allows personal freedom without giving away the commercial product.
